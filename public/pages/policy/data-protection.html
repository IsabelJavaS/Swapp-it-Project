Data Protection

How we protect and handle your personal information

1. Introduction

At SWAPP-IT, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our educational platform.

Data Controller: Fundación Alberto Motta, Programa ¡Supérate! San Miguelito

2. Data we collect

Basic personal data

  • Identification information: Full name, email address
  • Academic information: Educational institution, level of studies
  • Contact information: Phone number (optional)
  • Verification information: Photograph for identity verification

Usage data

  • Platform activity: Published products, completed transactions
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Preferences: Account settings, notification preferences

Transaction data

  • Purchase history: Products purchased, prices, dates
  • Payment information: Payment methods used (without storing sensitive data)
  • Communications: Messages between users, problem reports

3. Purposes of processing

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Account management: Create and maintain your user account
  • Facilitate transactions: Enable buying, selling, and exchanging products
  • Identity verification: Ensure user authenticity
  • Communication: Send important notifications about the platform
  • Service improvement: Analyze usage to improve experience
  • Legal compliance: Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Security: Prevent fraud and protect platform integrity

4. Legal basis for processing

The processing of your personal data is based on:

  • Consent: For the use of non-essential cookies and promotional communications
  • Contractual execution: To provide platform services
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our services and prevent fraud
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable legal obligations

5. Information sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Service providers: Companies that help us operate the platform (Firebase, Google Analytics)
  • Legal compliance: When required by law or competent authorities
  • Rights protection: To protect our rights, property, or security
  • Consent: When you have given your explicit consent

6. Data security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is transmitted and stored encrypted
  • Restricted access: Only authorized personnel can access the data
  • Continuous monitoring: We constantly monitor the security of our systems
  • Backups: We maintain secure backups of the data
  • Security updates: We keep our systems updated

7. Data retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described:

  • Account data: While your account is active and up to 3 years after inactivity
  • Transaction data: 7 years to comply with tax obligations
  • Communication data: 2 years from the last interaction
  • Security data: As required by law

8. Your rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have about you
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Restriction: Restrict the processing of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw your consent at any time

9. International transfers

Some of our service providers may be located outside Panama. In these cases:

  • We ensure that adequate protection guarantees exist
  • We use approved standard contractual clauses
  • We verify that the destination country has an adequate level of protection

10. Minors

Our platform is aimed at students aged 15 and above. For users under 18:

  • We require consent from parents or legal guardians
  • We implement additional protection measures
  • We limit the collection of sensitive data

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • A prominent notice on our website
  • An email to your registered address
  • A notification in the application
