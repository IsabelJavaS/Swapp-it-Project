Cookie Policy

Information about cookie usage on SWAPP-IT

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. These cookies help us improve your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and provide personalized functionality.

2. Types of cookies we use

Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of the website and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Session cookies: Keep your session active while browsing the platform
  • Security cookies: Protect against attacks and maintain site integrity
  • Authentication cookies: Verify your identity when you log in

Performance cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our website:

  • Analytics cookies: Collect information about website usage anonymously
  • Performance cookies: Measure website speed and efficiency

Functionality cookies

These cookies enhance website functionality:

  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Language cookies: Maintain your preferred language
  • Theme cookies: Remember if you prefer light or dark mode

3. Third-party cookies

We use third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze website traffic and usage
  • Firebase: For authentication and database
  • Google Fonts: To load website fonts
  • Font Awesome: To display icons

4. Cookie duration

  • Session cookies: Are deleted when you close the browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a specified period
  • Authentication cookies: Can last up to 30 days to maintain your session

5. How to manage cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser settings

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy and security > Cookies and site data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Cookie banner

When you visit our site for the first time, you will see a banner that allows you to accept or reject non-essential cookies.

6. Impact of disabling cookies

If you decide to disable cookies, please note that:

  • Limited functionality: Some site features may not work properly
  • Personalized experience: We won't be able to remember your preferences
  • Authentication: You may need to log in more frequently
  • Performance: The site may load more slowly

7. Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • A notice on our website
  • An email to registered users
  • A notification in the application
