1. General

The app provides a secure and user-friendly platform for students and parents (guardians) to buy, sell, or exchange second-hand school supplies such as textbooks, backpacks, and uniforms. It helps families save money by making the acquisition and disposal of these often-costly items easier, promotes sustainability by giving products a second life, and fosters collaboration within the educational community.

Return Policy

Users are required to report any product defects within 72 hours. If an issue is not reported within this time limit, Swapp-It will not be responsible for any claims.

Valid defects for return include:

  • Missing parts of the product (for example, buttons).
  • Water damage.
  • Electronics not turning on.
  • Missing pages (for books).
  • Stains.
  • Torn clothing.
  • Missing buttons.

If the product description already includes the damages or defects above, the buyer accepts the purchase and the item is not eligible for return.
